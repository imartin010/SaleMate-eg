## üìä Presentation Outline (15-minute stakeholder meeting)

### Slide 1: Executive Summary
- **Current State:** 7.2/10 UI, 7.3/10 UX, 6.5/10 Mobile
- **Key Issue:** Mobile experience needs critical improvements
- **Impact:** 30-40% slower task completion on mobile

### Slide 2: Business Impact
- **User Pain Points:**
  - Small touch targets (below 44x44px minimum)
  - Inconsistent mobile layouts
  - Poor mobile forms
- **Risk:** User churn, reduced competitiveness
- **Opportunity:** 40-50% mobile usability improvement

### Slide 3: Recommended Solution
- **Phase 1 (2-3 weeks):** Mobile foundation
  - Touch target standardization
  - Mobile layout conversion
  - Bottom sheet filters
  - Form optimization
- **ROI:** High - Quick wins, immediate impact

### Slide 4: Investment & Timeline
- **Phase 1:** 2-3 weeks, High ROI
- **Phase 2:** 4-6 weeks, Medium-High ROI
- **Phase 3:** 8-12 weeks, Medium ROI
- **Total:** 14-21 weeks for complete mobile-first transformation

### Slide 5: Success Metrics
- **Phase 1 Targets:**
  - 100% touch target compliance
  - 40-50% improvement in mobile task completion
  - All tables converted to mobile cards

### Slide 6: Next Steps
- Approve Phase 1 implementation
- Allocate development resources
- Set up mobile testing
- Track metrics before/after
